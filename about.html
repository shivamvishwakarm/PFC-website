About - PFC (Patna Fried Chicken)

How It All Started

The Aim

PFC is the acronym of Patna Fried Chicken And Pizza & Fried Chicken, is a Patna, Bihar based Fried Chicken Store. We opened the store to take a goal of making it a brand name of Bihar. It is a one year old established Store has Seven stores in all over Patna town. PFC started by Md Asif Ali who made this store very famous among Patnaties very soon. Secret herbs and spices played important role to make items delicious. Our intention is to make it a brand name and to soon popular it in all over India, later on in all over the world. Our Crispy chicken home-delivery facility has helped us in reaching to our customers. We provide fresh preparing delicious, outstanding taste of fried Chicken items at affordable prices.
